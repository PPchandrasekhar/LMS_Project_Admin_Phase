About

Learning Management System

Welcome to our Learning Management System (LMS). This platform is designed to provide students with a comprehensive online learning experience.

Features

  • Access to course materials and resources
  • Video lectures and tutorials
  • Assignment submission and tracking
  • Interactive schedule and calendar
  • Communication with instructors
  • Progress tracking and reporting

How to Use

Navigate through the sidebar to access different sections of your student panel:

  • Dashboard - Overview of your courses and recent activity
  • My Courses - View and manage your enrolled courses
  • Assignments - View and submit assignments
  • Schedule - View upcoming events and sessions
  • Materials - Download course materials
  • Videos - Watch course videos
  • Messages - Communicate with instructors and classmates
  • Settings - Update your profile and preferences

Support

If you need assistance, please visit the Contact page or reach out to your course instructors.
